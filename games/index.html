Tank and Planes

I know, I know. The game has a horrible name.


The game was made in 48 hours for the Kenney Game Jam 2021.


In the jam, you had to use free assets made on Kenney's website. The assets are super high quality and we're very pleasant to use.


It was made in the Godot engine, and actually, the source code is avaliable here on Github.

Rise of the Karens

Actually this game was made a long while ago; it had a, much worse, original version.


This game is... ok? It's not that great, but it's good to sit down for a while when you have no other games on your phone and no internet.


It uses GameJolt accounts for trophies, leaderboards, and it'll save your highscore.


I've got another version in the works, but I haven't worked on it for a long time and I doubt I'll ever finish it.
